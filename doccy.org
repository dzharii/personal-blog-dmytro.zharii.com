** Configuration Management

To reload configuration:
#+begin_src emacs-lisp
load-file emacs-init/init.el
#+end_src

** Publishing

To publish static HTML website:
#+begin_src emacs-lisp
(org-publish-all)
#+end_src

To force publish static HTML website:
#+begin_src emacs-lisp
(org-publish-all t)
#+end_src
